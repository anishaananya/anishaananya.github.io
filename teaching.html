Teaching & Outreach

Sharing knowledge and making STEM education accessible for all learners.

Chudar – Math Instructors & App Developers (2022–Present)

We volunteer at Chudar, an Indian nonprofit providing free quality education to under-resourced students through after-school programs. Our work focuses on improving math skills for K–12 learners in low-income communities.

We’ve taught weekly two-hour classes for groups of 30+ students preparing for India’s National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship Exam, which awards financial aid to high-achieving students from low-income families. Over 50% of students passed under our mentorship.

We also built MathBuddy, an offline Android app that lets students learn and practice math individually using curated question banks.

Dougherty Math Circle (2021–Present)

The Dougherty Math Circle is a student-led nonprofit engaging students from 10+ schools in advanced math and data science through problem-solving sessions, workshops, and competition prep.

As Curriculum Developers, we’ve designed problem sets and taught 50+ elementary and middle schoolers preparing for math contests. As Heads of AI, we launched a 6-week AI and Math workshop for 30 students, integrating coding and analytics into math problem solving.

Now as Presidents, we lead outreach and curriculum teams, connecting with local schools and expanding access to enrichment programs.

Sci-AI Club – Co-Founders & Co-Presidents (2022–Present)

We founded Sci-AI to make the applications of AI in science accessible to students. Over two years, we’ve taught 30 lectures on machine learning, created interactive coding notebooks, and led 15+ workshops that bring AI concepts to life.

Our outreach includes speaking at school assemblies to 100+ students, collaborating with SWE and Robotics clubs, and running “Intro to AI” workshops for middle schoolers. This fall, we’re teaching 25 students in our newest program focused on hands-on AI exploration.

Math Club – Co-Presidents (2021–Present)

As Co-Presidents of Math Club, we’ve led problem-solving sessions and developed competition prep materials across all four years of high school.

We also organized an 8-week Girls in STEM program to inspire elementary schoolers and a 4-week MOEMS preparation workshop for middle schoolers, encouraging early confidence in math.

About Us

We are both very passionate violinists and musicians, and we’ve been playing violin for over eleven years.

We both completed the Royal Conservatory of Music Level 10 certification and have been invited multiple times to perform in the California All-State and CODA Honors Orchestras .

Ananya most recently learned the Mendelssohn Violin Concerto, and Anisha learned the Sibelius Violin Concerto. Together, we performed Navarra, a fast-paced violin duet that showcases the dynamic conversation between our violins!

In addition to classical music, we love arranging and performing covers together — something we’ve done for as long as we can remember! We share our performances on our YouTube channel, A&A Violin Duo.

Anisha leads the music arranging, while Ananya handles the video editing and post-production. You can check out some of our performances below!
